# Day 4: Packet Sniffing and Wireshark

Today I moved into traffic analysis. I learned how to "listen" to the network to see exactly what data is being sent.

### Tools Used:
- Wireshark: A tool that allows me to capture and inspect packets in real-time.
-Filters: I practiced using filters to isolate specific IP addresses or protocols (e.g., `tcp.port == 443`).

### Observation:
I observed the "Encapsulation" process, where data from higher OSI layers is wrapped in headers from lower layers as it prepares to be sent.

### Active Recall:
- Q: What tool is used to see a "video replay" of network traffic?
- A: Wireshark.
